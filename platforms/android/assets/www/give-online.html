LCC Online Giving

Thank you so much for making a donation! It's our dream to help people in Lafayette discover the full life available to them in Christ, and your gifts make that dream possible.

We use PayPal for online donations. They will give you the option of using their payment system (PayPal account required), setting up an automatic withdrawal from your bank just like a check, or even using a credit card for recurring donations.

Monthly Recurring Gifts

PayPal will process the funds for us as something they call a "subscription" but it will still be registered with us as a tax-deductible donation.

Note: Enter your number without any $ before it.
